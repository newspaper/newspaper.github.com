The Bahamas is an island country situated in the Atlantic Ocean. It’s one of the biggest Caribbean nations with over 700 islands. It’s famous because of its tourism spots. The Bahamas is one of the richest countries in The Americas standing only behind America and Canada. There are numerous luxurious cruises setting sail from Florida to The Bahamas. The official language of Bahamas is English. Official Gazette: The Bahamas is the official newspaper of the Bahamas Government. There are lots of island based local newspapers in the Bahamas. The Bahamas has lots of online newspapers and magazines in the country.

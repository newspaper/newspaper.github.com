All Austria Newspapers

Austria is a central European country with a population of 8.5 million. The official language of Austria is German. Most of the daily newspapers in the country are published in German language. There are also newspapers published in English all over the country. There are also small number local newspapers in the country. There are a few of Turkish, Serbian and Croatian speaking groups in Austria. There are online and printed newspapers for those communities. There are a lot of online and printed Women Magazine, Fashion Magazine and Health magazine published in the country both in German and English.

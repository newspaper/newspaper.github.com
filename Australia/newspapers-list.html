Australia is the largest country in Australia continent and sixth largest country in the world. There is no official language in Australia. English is the most spoken language of Australia and the de facto official language. Australia has a lot of national and local newspaper and magazine agencies all over the country. There are lots of foreign immigrants from all over the world live in Australia. Due to that newspapers are published in many different languages. The Australian is the biggest countrywide daily newspaper in Australia. The Australian Financial Review is the largest financial and business newspaper in the country.

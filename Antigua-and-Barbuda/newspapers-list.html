All Antigua and Barbuda Newspapers

Antigua and Barbuda is a twin-island nation situated in the Caribbean Sea. Antigua and Barbudaare the two large inhabited islands of the country. With an estimated population over 90,000 Antigua and Barbuda is considered a small nation. English is the official language of the country. Daily Observer and the Antigua Sun were two daily newspapers in the country. In 2010 the Antigua Sun ceased functioning. There are a lot of magazines and online news portal in the country. Like most of the Caribbean natives, people in Antigua love their sports and music. There are many sports and music related magazines in the country.

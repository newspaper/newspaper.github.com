The Daily Prothom Alo Bangla Newspaper

"The daily Prothom Alo" is one of a largest circulated daily newspaper in Bangladesh (latitudes 20° and 27°N, and longitudes 88° and 93°E), was founded on 4 November 1998. "Prothom Alo" published from Dhaka in the Bengali language (with at least 3 million first-language speakers, Bangla is ranked sixth). "Prothom Alo" is bengali word which means "First Light". This leading daily newspaper owned by Transcom Group. Prothom Alo provides trusted Bangladesh and International news as well as local and regional perspectives.

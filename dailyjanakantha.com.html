About Daily Janakantha

Daily Janakantha is one of the most popular daily newspaper in Bangladesh which published from Dhaka, Bangladesh since 21 February 1993 in bengali language. Owned by the Globe group also known as Globe Janakantha Shilpa Paribar. Mohammad Atikullah Khan is the editor of Daily Janakantha. It has its own set up of local press and offices which located in Banglamotor, Dhaka. Early in the morning dainik janakantha distributed all over Bangladesh.

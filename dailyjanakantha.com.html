The Daily Janakantha Bangla Newspaper

The Daily Janakantha is one of the most popular daily newspaper with highest circulation which published from Dhaka, Bangladesh in bengali language. It is owned by the Globe group also known as Globe Janakantha Shilpa Paribar. This newspaper was first published on 21 February 1993 With the slogan "No compromise in the question of principles". The Daily Janakantha is the only daily newspaper in Bangladesh which is simultaneously printed and published from 5 different regions of the country through microwave system with its own set-up of local press and offices and distributed early in the morning all over Bangladesh.

All Andorra Newspapers

Andorra is a Catalan speaking country located between Spain and France. It’s a small sovereign state with the population of mere 85,000. There are 4 major ethinic groups in the country consisting of Andorran, Spanish, Portuguese and French. Catalan is recognized as the official language of the country. But there are other languages Portuguese, Spanish and French. Most of the newspaper and magazine is written in Catalan language. The Spanish and Portuguese speaking community might have their own brand of newspaper but they can easily read and understand Catalan. Catalan, Spanish and Portuguese are basically the same language. There are also web portal serving as English newspaper and magazines for the country.

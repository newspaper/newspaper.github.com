All Argentina Newspapers

Argentina is a South American country. Argentina is the 8th largest country in the world by mass, second in Latin America and largest Spanish speaking country. Spanish is the official language of Argentina. Clarin is the largest newspaper publisher in the country. Most of the country’s newspapers are published in Spanish language. But there are also foreign language newspapers in the country. The foreign language newspapers include English, German and French. The country is notable for its National Football team. Argentina is famous for football and there are lots of sports newspaper and magazines in the country of Argentina. There are also lots of online news websites both in English and Spanish.

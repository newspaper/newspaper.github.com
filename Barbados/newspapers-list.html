All Barbados Newspapers

Barbados is an island country located in the Caribbean. It is a small island with 34 km length and 24 km width are. It’s the most popular country of West Indies. The official language of Barbados is English. All form of written documents and laws in Barbados are written in English. The country’s newspapers are also written in English. Barbados has been popular with cricket for a long and has a lot of sports magazines published. The people of Barbados enjoy their music and has music related magazines too. The pop star Rihanna was born in Barbados.

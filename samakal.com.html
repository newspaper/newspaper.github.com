Samakal Bangla Newspaper

"The daily Samakal" (দৈনিক সমকাল) which is a Bengali language daily newspaper and published in Dhaka, Bangladesh(latitudes 20° and 27°N, and longitudes 88° and 93°E). It started publishing from 31 May, 2005 to till now. "Samakal" is bengali word which means "Current Time". Golam Sarwar is the editor of this newspaper. And A.K. Azad is the publisher of this samakal newspaper. Maj. Gen. S.M. Shahab Uddin act as Executive Director. Abu Sayeed Khan is the Managing Editor of daily samakal and Abed Khan was also work here as editor. Famous columnists Abdul Gaffar Chowdhury contributes every week in `Kaler Ayna' of this newspaper. "Kaler Kheya" is bengali word which means "Boat of the Time" is the most popular literary magazine of this time and also one of the weekly supplements of this newspaper. This leading daily samakal newspaper owned by Times Media Limited, Ha-meem Group.

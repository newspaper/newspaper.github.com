Samakal Bangla Newspaper

"The daily Samakal" (দৈনিক সমকাল) is one of a bengali language daily newspaper in Bangladesh (latitudes 20° and 27°N, and longitudes 88° and 93°E), was founded on April 2005 and started publishing in 31 May, 2005. "Samakal" is bengali word which means "Current Time". This leading daily newspaper owned by Times Media Limited, Ha-meem Group.

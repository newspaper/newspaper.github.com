All Belarus Newspapers

Belarus is a landlocked country in Eastern Europe. Belarusian and Russian are recognized as official languages of Belarus. Belarusian is the national language of Belarus. There are privately owned and state owned newspaper publishers in the country. There are a large number of newspapers published in Russian language. 72% of the people speak Russian in Belarus. Belarusian language newspapers are also published in abundance. There are a huge number of online newspapers in Belarus. Most of the English newspapers are published in both Belarusian and Russian language. Most of the newspapers are published in Minsk.
